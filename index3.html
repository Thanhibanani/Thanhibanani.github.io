Our Team

Christoffer Gård

CIO (Chief Innovation Officer)

I am interested in the intersection between technology and business, especially how technology can create sustainability and value for companies. This interest has influenced my work, where I involved myself deeply in how people use technology in their everyday life. Through guiding customers on their journey to become technologically literate in an ever-changing landscape I have firsthand experience with the value technology can provide. My experiences with humans and technology further influenced my choice of master thesis, where I wrote about how leaders can influence followers’ mindset and self-efficacy. Seeing that digitization processes can be difficult for many, one has to start with the mindset of the individual and their approach to learning and personal growth.

Ola Sandberg Iversen

CMO - Chief Marketing Officer

Technology is like magic, it brings screens alive and makes us able to have a conversation with someone halfway around the globe. Except it isn’t magic. It’s man made, and has the potential to better the lives of humans worldwide. I want to be able to create “magic”, to innovate and bring positive change, to develop technology and solutions that contribute to better the standard of living for people.
