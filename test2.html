The Product

Introducing the first product from the Chameleon Clothing company, the color changing shoe.
Made of natural and eco-friendly fabrics carefully sourced from high quality manufacturers , the shoe itself is a comfortable blessing for your feet. But the superior quality is just a nice side effect to the shoe's main selling point; it can change color. With cutting edge LED-technology wrapped around the shoe, the customer can switch its look from one moment to the next.

You felt like wearing white sneakers when you woke up, but now it's lunch time and you want to mix things up?
Just use the color-slider on the app and the shoe transforms. In a world where unsustainable use of resources are a big problem, and a fashion sector where new trends and products are pumped out left and right. We believe that one product which can fill many roles and never gets unfashionable is a step in the right direction.
